Prime Martial Arts · White Belt Tour

Three prototypes of the new student orientation tour. All cover the same 10 orientation questions. Try each one and see which feels right.

Version A

The Belt Path

A guided, scrolling tour. Seven stops, each with drone footage and its orientation questions. Progress fills in belt by belt as you answer.

 Version B

Explore the School

A fullscreen walkthrough. Move room to room and tap the markers to open each spot's video and questions. Answer all ten to finish.

 Version C

The Flight

The drone flies you from stop to stop. Land at each one, answer its questions, then take off for the next.

Prototypes - Prime Martial Arts, Lawrence KS
